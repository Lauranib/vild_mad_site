Educational material

Welcome to Vild Mad, where we are passionate about educating and inspiring individuals to connect with nature through wild food exploration. Our educational materials aim to enrich your understanding of the diverse array of edible plants and the art of foraging responsibly.

Embark on a journey of discovery with our comprehensive wild food curriculum. Designed for learners of all levels, our courses cover everything from plant identification to sustainable foraging practices. Whether you're a novice or an experienced forager, our curriculum caters to your curiosity and deepens your connection to the natural world.

Our engaging lesson plans make learning about wild food fun and interactive. Designed for both classroom settings and individual study, these lessons incorporate multimedia elements, hands-on activities, and real-life foraging experiences. We believe that hands-on learning is the key to fostering a lifelong appreciation for the bounty of nature.

Participate in our interactive workshops to enhance your practical foraging skills. Led by experienced foragers and educators, these workshops offer a hands-on approach to learning. Whether you join us in person or virtually, you'll gain valuable knowledge and practical tips for incorporating wild foods into your lifestyle.

Tips for teachers

Dear Educators, Welcome to Vild Mad, your go-to resource for integrating the wonders of wild food into your classroom curriculum. As you embark on this exciting journey of exploration with your students, here are some tips to maximize the impact of our educational materials:

Our wild food curriculum is flexible and adaptable. Tailor the content to align with your existing curriculum and educational goals. Whether you're teaching biology, environmental science, or even culinary arts, there's a wealth of knowledge waiting to be discovered in the world of wild edibles.

Bring lessons to life by incorporating multimedia elements from our educational materials. From vibrant plant identification visuals to captivating foraging experiences, multimedia can capture your students' attention and make learning about wild food an immersive experience.

Encourage experiential learning through hands-on activities. Consider organizing outdoor foraging trips or hands-on workshops to complement the theoretical knowledge gained from our materials. Engaging all the senses in the learning process can deepen students' understanding and appreciation for the natural world.
